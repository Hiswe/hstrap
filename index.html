Overview

Hstrap is a little lib proposing mixins & classes build on top of Stylus & Nib

It will not be a complete framework as twitter bootstrap, fluidity or roots.

The purpose is more to give some uncommon mixins and provide some particular design classes for buttons and forms.

The less is provided, the better you can design without extra markup or classes.

Provided

All in seperate files: include what you want

  • Buttons
  • Forms
  • Flexbox Grid

Will be removed in the futur (I think)

  • Nib reset
  • Very minimal typography (only headings)
